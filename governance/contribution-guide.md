## How To Contribute

Open a pull request with a focused change. Keep claims checkable, include sources for factual claims, and explain what the change improves.

## Content Rules

- Teach questions, not loyalty.
- Avoid secret-knowledge framing.
- Include uncertainty where appropriate.
- Prefer plain language.
- Avoid party-political endorsements in core material.

## Review

Contributions should be checked against the review checklist before they are merged.
