## Principles

- Evidence beats loyalty.
- Claims must be checkable.
- Criticism is not betrayal.
- No gurus.
- No secret knowledge.
- No purity tests.
- No violence.
- No dehumanisation.
- No party endorsements in core material.
- Always include counterarguments and boring explanations where relevant.

## Editorial Test

If a page makes people more certain but not more careful, revise it.
