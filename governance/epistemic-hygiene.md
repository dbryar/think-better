## Core Questions

- What would change our mind?
- How do we handle corrections?
- How do we distinguish scepticism from paranoia?
- How do we prevent becoming a conspiracy pipeline?
- How do we avoid becoming a cult or party front?

## Correction Practice

Corrections should be visible, specific, and boring. Name what changed, why it changed, and what evidence supported the change.

## Scepticism Boundary

The project can criticise institutions without teaching that all institutions are fake, corrupt, or beyond repair.
