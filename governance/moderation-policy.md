## Not Allowed

- Harassment.
- Hate.
- Threats.
- Recruitment into extremist, conspiratorial, cultic, or violent ideologies.
- Spam.
- Bad-faith flooding.
- Unverifiable claims presented as fact.
- Medical, legal, or financial advice presented beyond general literacy unless properly sourced and scoped.

## Moderation Goal

The goal is not to protect the project from criticism. The goal is to keep the work usable, safe, and method-based.
