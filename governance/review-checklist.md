## Review Questions

- Could this framing be reused by a sovcit, cult recruiter, extremist, or grifter with only nouns changed?
- Does it teach a method rather than demand belief?
- Does it include uncertainty where appropriate?
- Are factual claims sourced?
- Does it punch down?
- Does it make a totalising claim?
- Does it invite correction?
- Does it avoid secret-knowledge framing?
- Does it avoid "chosen few" language?
- Does it help users become harder to manipulate?

## Decision

If the answer is unclear, keep the page in draft and ask for another reviewer.
