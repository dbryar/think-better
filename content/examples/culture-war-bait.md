## Scenario

A city is debating rent increases, vacancy rates, and building approvals. Suddenly, the loudest story becomes a misleading clip from a school board meeting.

## What Happens

People argue about identity, loyalty, and disgust. The rent debate continues with less attention. The misleading clip gets monetised by accounts that post outrage every day.

## Check The Frame

- Is the school story real, exaggerated, or invented?
- Why is this story receiving more attention than the housing decision?
- Who benefits if people stop watching the rent debate?
- Are people being asked to hate a group instead of understand a system?

## Better Habit

You can care about social issues and still ask whether your attention is being redirected.
