## Keep It Method-Based

Use fictional or historical examples when current partisan examples would pull the class into team defence.

## Assessment Ideas

- Identify the claim and evidence separately.
- Find one missing context question.
- Write the strongest counterargument.
- Explain what evidence would change your view.

## Safeguard

Do not ask students to disclose personal political beliefs.
