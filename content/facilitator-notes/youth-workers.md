## Start With Agency

Young people are often targeted because they are isolated, angry, or looking for status. Treat them as thinkers, not empty containers.

## Watch For Recruitment Patterns

Be alert when a group offers certainty, belonging, and an enemy while discouraging outside relationships or questions.

## Keep The Door Open

If someone is already deep in a manipulative community, direct confrontation can push them closer to it. Keep conversations calm, specific, and repeatable.
