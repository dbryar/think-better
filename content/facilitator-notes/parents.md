## Use Questions First

Ask young people what they notice before telling them what you notice. The goal is not to win a debate. The goal is to build habits they can use without you present.

## Avoid Humiliation

Do not mock someone for believing a bad claim. Shame makes people defend the claim harder.

## Useful Prompts

- What part feels most convincing?
- What would you need to check?
- Who benefits if people share this quickly?
- What would the boring explanation be?
