A mark is not stupid. A mark is someone whose emotions, identity, loneliness, anger, fear, hope, or certainty are being used by someone else.

Grifters rarely start by asking for everything. They start by offering certainty, belonging, and an enemy.

## The Package

Manipulative movements often bundle three things:

- Certainty: "We know what is really happening."
- Belonging: "You are one of the people smart enough to see it."
- Enemy: "Those people are why your life is hard."

## The Escape Hatch

Before accepting the package, ask:

- What would change my mind?
- Can I question leaders without punishment?
- Am I being asked to cut off outside sources?
- Is anger being used before evidence?
- Who benefits from my loyalty?

## Keep Your Exit Open

Any group worth trusting can survive your questions.
