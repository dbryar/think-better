## Core Questions

- Could this be explained by money, incentives, habit, bureaucracy, fear, incompetence, or career risk?
- Is a secret conspiracy necessary to explain it?
- Are ordinary failures being dressed up as hidden coordination?

## Better Habit

Look for ordinary incentives before assuming hidden orchestration.
