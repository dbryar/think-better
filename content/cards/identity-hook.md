## Core Questions

- Am I being told what "people like us" believe?
- Is disagreement treated as betrayal?
- Is the claim offering certainty, belonging, and an enemy in one package?
- Can I reject this claim without losing the group?

## Better Habit

Healthy groups can handle careful disagreement.
