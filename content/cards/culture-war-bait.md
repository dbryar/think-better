## Core Questions

- Why this story, why now?
- Is the issue real, exaggerated, or invented?
- What material problem is being ignored while we fight about this?
- Are people being asked to hate a group instead of understand a system?

## Better Habit

Take the issue seriously enough to ask whether the attention around it is being used honestly.
