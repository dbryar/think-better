## Core Questions

- Does this claim leave room for being wrong?
- What evidence would change the speaker's mind?
- Are doubt and questions treated as betrayal?
- Is uncertainty being mocked rather than addressed?

## Better Habit

Confidence is not evidence. Good explanations can survive careful questions.
