## Core Questions

- What is this person actually qualified in?
- Are they speaking inside or outside their field?
- Do they show their evidence?
- Do other credible experts disagree?
- Are they selling something?

## Better Habit

Do not replace blind trust with blind distrust. Ask whether the claimed authority matches the claim being made.
