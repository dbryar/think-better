## Core Questions

- Is the blame being aimed at people with less power?
- Are the people causing the problem missing from the story?
- Does the proposed solution punish the visible, not the powerful?
- Is this issue being used to avoid talking about money, ownership, wages, rent, tax, regulation, or accountability?

## Warning Sign

If the story needs you to hate people with less power, slow down and ask who is absent from the frame.
