## Core Questions

- Who gains money, power, attention, status, votes, or protection if people believe this?
- Who loses if people stop believing it?
- Is the claimed enemy actually powerful, or just convenient?
- What would this claim make me ignore?

## Use It When

Use this card when a claim arrives with urgency, outrage, certainty, or a neat villain. Incentives do not prove a claim false, but they help you decide where to look next.

## Better Habit

Ask who benefits before asking which side you are supposed to join.
