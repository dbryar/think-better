## Core Questions

- Am I doing free work for someone's campaign, grift, brand, church, faction, or media outlet?
- Would the person who made this benefit from my outrage?
- Am I spreading a message I have not checked?
- Am I being farmed?

## Better Habit

Your attention is useful. Spend it deliberately.
