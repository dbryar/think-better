## Core Questions

- Am I sharing this because it is true, or because it feels satisfying?
- Has the source shown the full context?
- Is the headline doing more work than the evidence?
- Would I share this if it attacked my own side?
- Who benefits from me spreading it?

## Pause

Waiting five minutes is not weakness. It is a way to avoid becoming unpaid distribution for someone else's agenda.
